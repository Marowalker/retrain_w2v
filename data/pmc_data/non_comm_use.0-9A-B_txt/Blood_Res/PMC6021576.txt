==== Body
This retracts the article “Primary CNS high-grade B-cell lymphoma, with rearrangements of MYC and BCL6: a case report” in volume 53 on page 87.

Blood Res 2018 Mar;53(1):87-89.

https://doi.org/10.5045/br.2018.53.1.87

This article has been retracted following a review by the Editorial Board. It has come to light that there is a significant overlap between the article and one that had already appeared in Baylor University Medical Center Proceedings (Published online: 09 May 2018, DOI: 10.1080/08998280.2018.1460127).
