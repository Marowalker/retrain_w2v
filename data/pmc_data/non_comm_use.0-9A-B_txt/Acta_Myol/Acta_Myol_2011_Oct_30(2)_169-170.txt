MSM
The 10th Congress of the Mediterranean Society of Myology was held in Pisa from 28 to 30 April 2011. The proceedings of the Meeting are published in the current issue (pages 149-166).

During the Congress, a new board was elected. Giovanni Nigro was confirmed as President; Lefkos Middleton and George Serratrice as Vice-Presidents; Yeuda Shapira as Secretary; Luisa Politano as Treasurer; Ekram Abdel Salam, Marinos Dalakas, Feyza Deymeer, Fayçal Hentati, Giovanni Meola, Gabriele Siciliano, Eduardo Tizzano, Antonio Toscano, Janez Zidar as Members. Valerie Askanas, Salvatore di Mauro, King Engel, Irena Hausmanowa-Petrusewicz, Reinhard Rüdel as co-opted Members.

The 11th Congress of the Mediterranean Society of Myology will take place in Marseille, in 2013 in a joint meeting with the "Societé NeuroMediterranée" chaired by Professor George Serratrice.

WMS
The 16th International WMS Congress will take place in Algarve (Portugal) in October 2011. The Conference will follow the newly introduced 4-day format from Wednesday 19th through Saturday 22nd October, with an Opening Reception and Special Lecture on Tuesday evening 18th October. The symposium will be in the traditional WMS format with 3 selected topics:

Duchenne and Becker Muscular Dystrophy and associated Dystrophinopathies.

New developments in Spinal Muscular Atrophies and Hereditary Neuropathies.

Advances in Therapy of Neuromuscular Disorders

Contributions will also be welcome on New Advances across the Neuromuscular field.

One day of the symposium will be dedicated to each of the three selected topics. Invited keynote speakers will summarize the state of the art on the selected topics, covering clinical, molecular and other aspects. The sessions will comprise contributed oral papers and poster presentations with guided discussions.

The 17th International WMS Congress will take place in Perth – Australia – 13-19 October 2012.
