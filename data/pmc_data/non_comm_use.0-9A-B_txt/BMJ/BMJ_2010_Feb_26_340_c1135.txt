In this letter by John C Stevenson and colleagues about the women’s health initiative (WHI) trial (BMJ 2010;340:c591, doi:10.1136/bmj.c591), the difference in ovarian cancer incidence between hormone replacement therapy and placebo was, as we said, non-significant, but we then wrongly added a P value of 0.02 during editing.
