In this paper by Levis and colleagues (BMJ 2019;361:l1476, doi:10.1136/bmj.l1476, published 9 April 2019), the names of the members of the DEPRESsion Screening Data (DEPRESSD) Collaboration were omitted. This was due to a processing error and has now been corrected.
