==== Body
In the conclusion section of this Research paper (BMJ 2016;355:i5044, doi:10.1136/bmj.i5044), the authors should have said that, “Delivery by scheduled cesarean delivery should be delayed until 39 weeks 0 days to 39 weeks 6 days whenever possible to reduce the risk of respiratory morbidity.” We apologise for any confusion this may have caused.
