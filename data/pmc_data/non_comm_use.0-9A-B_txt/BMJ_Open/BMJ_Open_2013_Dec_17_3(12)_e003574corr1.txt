Millard LAC, Lawlor DA, Fraser A, et al. Physical activity during pregnancy and offspring cardiovascular risk factors: findings from a prospective cohort study. BMJ Open 2013;3:e003574. The funding and competing interests sections were published incorrectly and should be as follows:

Funding The work undertaken in this paper was specifically funded by a grant from the US NIH (R01 DK077659). The UK Medical Research Council and the Wellcome Trust (Grant ref: 092731) and the University of Bristol provide core support for ALSPAC. The UK Medical Research Council (MC_UU_12013/5) and the University of Bristol provide funding for the MRC Integrative Epidemiology Unit.

Competing interests LACM is funded by a studentship from the UK Medical Research Council. AF and LDH are funded by a UK Medical Research Council research fellowship (G0701594, and G1002375, respectively).
