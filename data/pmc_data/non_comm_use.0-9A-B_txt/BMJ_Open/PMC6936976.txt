==== Body
Adrian M, Borgquist O, Bentzer P, et al. Research protocol for mechanical complications after central venous catheterisation: a prospective controlled multicentre observational study to determine incidence and risk factors of mechanical complications within 24 hours after cannulation. BMJ Open 2019;9:e029301. doi: 10.1136/bmjopen-2019-029301

The article was previously published with errors.

Reference no. 5 was incorrect in the published version. The correct reference is

5. Polderman KH, Girbes ARJ. Central venous catheter use. Part 2: infectious complications. Intensive Care Med 2002;28:18–28. doi:10.1007/s00134-001-1156-7 [published Online First: 29 November 2001]

Reference no. 8 was omitted in the published version. The correct reference is

8. Polderman KH, Girbes ARJ. Central venous catheter use. Part 1: mechanical compliactions. Intensive care Med 2002;28:1-17. doi: 10.1007/s00134-001-1154-9. [published Online First: 4 December 2001]

Reference no. 5 is only cited once (introduction section, second paragraph). Reference no. 8 is cited twice (introduction section, third paragraph and ethics and dissemination section, second paragraph).
