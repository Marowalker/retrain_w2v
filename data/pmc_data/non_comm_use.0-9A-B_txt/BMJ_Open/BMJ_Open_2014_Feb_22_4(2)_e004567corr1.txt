Bone A, Mc Grath-Lone L, Day S, et al. Inequalities in the care experiences of patients with cancer: analysis of data from the National Cancer Patient Experience Survey 2011–2012. BMJ Open 2014;4:e004567. The sentence in the Objectives section of the Abstract should read: ‘To explore inequalities in the care experiences of patients with cancer by patient, clinical and trust-level factors.’ In addition, the second line of the Discussion section should read: ‘Women, younger patients, ethnic minorities and patients with a long-standing condition or disability were less likely to rate their cancer care as ‘excellent’ or ‘very good’.’
