Halford C, Welin C, Bogefeldt J, et al. A population-based study of
nearly 15 000 observations among Swedish women and men during 1973–2003.
BMJ Open 2012;2:e001353. Part of the article title was erroneously omitted. The
full title should read ‘Effects of age and secular trends on self-rated health. A
population-based study of nearly 15 000 observations among Swedish women and men during
1973–2003’.
