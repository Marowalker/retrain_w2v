==== Body
Heuch I, Heuch I, Hagen K, et al. The association between body height and chronic low back pain: a follow-up in the Nord-Trøndelag Health Study. BMJ Open 2015;5:e006983.

In table 1 of this paper, the number of men in the Height (cm) 175–179 group, ‘Recurrence among persons with LBP at baseline’, is not correct. The correct number is 774 (not 74).
