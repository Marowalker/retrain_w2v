This article was previously published with an error.

The funding information in the published article was incomplete. The complete funding details are as follows:

This study is supported by unrestricted public grants from Conseil Régional du Nord Pas-de-Calais and from caregivers Ligue National contre le Cancer. The EPIC trial (NCT02853474.) is also supported by public grants from the French National Cancer Institute, INCa (INCa-DGOS_11170). The funders have no role in study design, management, analysis and interpretation of data as well as no role in the writing of the final report.

These errors did not affect the design or results of the study.
