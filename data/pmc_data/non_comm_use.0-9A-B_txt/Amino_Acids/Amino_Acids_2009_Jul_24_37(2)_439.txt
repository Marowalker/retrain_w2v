Erratum to: Amino Acids (2006) 30:369–389 DOI 10.1007/s00726-005-0283-9
In the published original version of this article, the information in the following sentence is not given correct: p. 371, left column, second paragraph, 4th line.

The correct sentence should read:

Beta A1 and A3 crystallin are encoded by the same gene, translation of the mRNA is initiated from distinct start codons, the beta A3 start codon being upstream to A1.

The online version of the original article can be found under doi:10.1007/s00726-005-0283-9.
