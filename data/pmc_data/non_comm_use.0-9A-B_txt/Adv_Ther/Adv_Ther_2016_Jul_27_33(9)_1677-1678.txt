Erratum to: Adv Ther (2016) 33:1012–1024 DOI 10.1007/s12325-016-0335-4
Following publication of the aforementioned article the authors noticed that a sentence had been omitted from the acknowledgements section pertaining to the AstraZeneca development team. The acknowledgements section should read as follows:

The online version of the original article can be found under doi:10.1007/s12325-016-0335-4.

ACKNOWLEDGMENTS
