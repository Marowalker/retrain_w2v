==== Body
In our article, “Methylaluminoxane-Free
Chromium Catalytic System for Ethylene Tetramerization”, we
unfortunately neglected the citation of the relevant patents. We regret
this mistake and would like to give the inventors the credit they
deserve. The revised final sentence in the conclusion with the citations
added is given below.

Original sentence: However, the [B(C6F5)4]− anion in itself
is compatible with trialkylaluminum
once it is not paired with a trityl cation; thus, [(PNP)CrCl2(CH3CN)2]+[B(C6F5)4]−/Et3Al (or iB3Al) exhibited high activity.

Corrected sentence: However,
the [B(C6F5)4]− anion in itself is compatible with trialkylaluminum
once it is not paired with a trityl cation; thus, [(PNP)CrCl2(CH3CN)2]+[B(C6F5)4]−/Et3Al (or iB3Al) exhibited high activity, and the catalytic system comprised
of ammonium borate ([R3NH]+[B(C6F5)4]−) instead of trityl borate
was successful.60−62
