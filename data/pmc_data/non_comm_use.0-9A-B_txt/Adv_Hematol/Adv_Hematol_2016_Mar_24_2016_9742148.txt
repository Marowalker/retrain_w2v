The article titled “Plasmablastic Lymphoma: A Review of Current Knowledge and Future Directions” [1] has been retracted as it was found to contain a substantial amount of material from the following published article: “Plasmablastic Lymphoma: A Systematic Review” by Jorge J. Castillo and John L. Reagan, in The Scientific World Journal.
